Why Clever Cloud is Different?

Cloud infrastructures often lead to a feeling of working in a black box. Have a look at how Clever Cloud provides all the tools and features you need.

Services

Deploy with Git

Use your favorite deployment tools (Git or FTP). Integrate with your favorite continuous integration/delivery solutions

Get Managed Services

Clever Cloud offer a large set of services. Pick the best fit for your apps, and configure add-ons with one click for your databases and other kind of services

Intelligent Auto-scaling

Choose freely the amount of resources you need. Configure your own scaling scenarios, just in case you'll get unexpected trafic or usage

Obsvervability

Slack integration

Work with Slack ? With one click, integrate your deployment workflow in your favorite channel to follow each new release your team is shipping.

Github login

Github-based authentication (both repositories and SSH keys). Auto-redeployment on push. No codebase duplication Environment variables to avoid sensitive data sharing.

Built-in Security

From automatic SSL certificates to automatic kernel and software updates, get the most secure environment without any required action

Security

Active Monitoring

Everything, from hardware to apps is auto-monitored and analyzed. You get real-time apps resources usage in Metrics and you can insert your own business data points to correlate impacts.

Auto Healing of Apps

Auto-Healing: apps are restarted/redeployed if they get too slow or crash. Clever Cloud caches your build for faster recovery of your apps.

Command Line Tools

Besides our web console, we have open-sourced our cli, Clever Tools : it allows you to manage your deployment lifecycle / configuration within your terminal.
